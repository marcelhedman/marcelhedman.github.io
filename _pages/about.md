I am a DPhil candidate in Statistical Machine Learning at the University of Oxford, supported by the [StatML](https://statml.io/) fellowship and supervised by Professor Chris Holmes and Professor Tom Rainforth. My research focuses on **sequential decision making**, with particular interests in **multi-agent reinforcement learning**, **world modelling** and **Bayesian experimental design**.

Previously, I read Natural Sciences (Physics) at the University of Cambridge and was a Choate Memorial Fellow at Harvard University, where I worked on data science at the intersection of machine learning and neurodegenerative disease. Before returning to academia, I was in the founding team at [Axion Ray](https://axionray.com/), applying AI to advanced industries, where amongst other things, I built the initial product-lead team and led deployments across enterprise accounts. Outside of research, I run Nural, an early-stage AI fund.
